Our Story

How It All Began

Every great love story starts somewhere. Ours started with an Instagram story... and a very strategic man.

Every great love story starts somewhere. Ours started with an Instagram story... and a very strategic man.

One day, Ofure was scrolling through Instagram when he came across a birthday post featuring Somadina. The post included her Instagram handle and a birthday message. There was just one small problem: by the time he saw it, her birthday had already passed by a day.

Now, a normal person would have sent a DM.

But Ofure clearly had other plans.

Instead of messaging her directly, he contacted a baker, shared her Instagram handle, and arranged for a birthday cake to be delivered to her. As fate would have it, Somadina had just received a call from a friend saying they had ordered a cake for her and that someone would be reaching out soon. So when the “cake delivery” message arrived, she naturally assumed it was from her friend and happily shared her address.

A little while later, the cake arrived with a note attached:

“Hey beautiful, I missed your birthday, but you still deserve a cake. Not just because of your birthday, but because of your beauty.”

And, of course, there was a phone number attached.

Surprised and impressed, Somadina sent a text to say thank you. Almost immediately, his reply came:

“You're welcome. But now I think I deserve two things: a follow-back and a chance to make you smile in person.”

Cheeky? Yes. Effective? Also yes.

Not long after, Ofure invited her to a house party he was hosting. On the day of the party, Somadina was out with friends at the beach and expected to be out late. Ofure simply replied, “Okay, text me when you're back.”

After a full day in the sun, her friends decided to continue the fun at a club. But Somadina wasn't quite in the clubbing mood. Then she remembered the house party invitation and sent him a text.

Almost instantly, he replied, like someone who had been waiting for that message all day.

The party was still on, and before she knew it, he was offering to send a driver to pick her up. Thoughtful points were adding up quickly.

When she arrived, the house was full of people, music, and excitement. But somehow, despite all the activity, Ofure took her hand and led her straight past the crowd to the balcony.

And what a balcony it was.

The view was breathtaking, but before long, neither of them paid much attention to it. They talked, laughed, shared stories, and got to know each other. While the party carried on inside, they remained outside, completely engrossed in conversation.

Hours passed without either of them noticing.

It wasn't until a friend called to check on her that Somadina realized it was already 3:30 a.m.

At that point, she knew it was time to head home.

Naturally, Ofure insisted on taking her himself.

And somewhere between Victoria Island and Chevron, between the conversations, laughter, and effortless connection, two strangers started becoming something more.

The rest, as they say, is history.

And now, here we are. ❤️
